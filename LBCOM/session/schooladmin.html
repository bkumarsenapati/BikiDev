School Administrators


We share the concern of School Administrators of failing students. To help Schools to cope with the struggling students, Learnbeyond through our Summer School Credit Recovery solution, provides full-semester courses based on Common Core State Standards and national best practices. These courses accommodate below grade, on grade, and/or above grade instruction by providing districts a way to provide an effective alternate method of delivering summer school. Since the classes take place online, parents do not need to provide transportation for their child and/or interrupt family schedules. For students that do not have access to a computer or internet, the district can facilitate a computer lab for those students.The program reduces the costs of opening and maintaining school space during the summer.


We can help the schools to reduce the dropout rate and increase the graduation rate which in turn help schools retain their budgets. To help the process rolling at your school, please contact creditrecovery@learnbeyond.com or
