Learnbeyond Privacy Policy


Please read the following privacy policy to know how much attention we pay at your privacy and how do we use your personally identifiable information that we collect from you in our site.

Information Collection and Use

Students of grades K-12 are the main users of Learnbeyond. We collect the name, address, phone number, date of birth, gender, email address of their parent and school information from students of grades K-12. We will collect all this information directly from the child. We are not using any cookies in collecting child information.

Learnbeyond uses the information of the users to customize the content you see, and to fulfill your requests in the products ONLY. We will not disclose the personal information that we collect from the users to any third parties.

We will obtain a verifiable parental consent from the child's parent when we take the personal information from the child by sending a mail to the parent.

Revoking & Deleting

In case, if any parent wants to review the child's personal information, revoke his/her consent, or wants to delete the information from our database, they can always contact us at the below mentioned address and can ask us to do so.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Learnbeyond may change the privacy policy from time to time. If we make any changes in the way that we use your personal identifiable information we will inform you by posting the prominent announcements on our site.
