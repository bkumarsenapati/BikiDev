Parents/ Students

Learnbeyon's K12 Credit Recovery program provides flexible, efficient and cost-effective way to earn credits for students in grades 6-12 for failed courses.


Students can take credit recovery classes completely online. Our School-affiliated certified teachers will guide the students, monitor and evaluate student's activities and work.


If you are or know a student who needs to sign up for Learnbeyond K12 credit recovery program this summer, you need to


  • Complete the online Request for Sign-Up form
  • Discuss with the student's guidance counselor at school to determine the type of course the student need to take and inform about this course
  • Learnbeyond representative can discuss with your School Counselor, if required
