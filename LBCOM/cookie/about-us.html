About Us

Learnbeyond is a single platform that integrates the best of technology, interface, and content to provide an unparalleled online learning experience. The platform was initially conceived as hotschools.net, an exam engine to help students in getting through highly competitive college admissions tests.

Since gaining popularity amongst the student community for its usefulness and effectiveness, the platform has evolved to its current state and has been rebranded as Learnbeyond. Built by a team of developers and project managers with a genuine passion for learning, Learnbeyond has come to be known as the standard in eLearning by its users today.

The Learnbeyond system is currently being used in several schools across the country as their go-to Virtual School to deliver K-12 classes completely online. The Learnbeyond Learning Management System Platform is also used in some schools for Blended Learning, Credit Recovery, Summer School and After-School programs.

Management Team

The company behind the product, also known as Learnbeyond, is the brain child of a visionary team of people from the fields of information technology and education. Their collective experience in eLearning amounts to more than a 100 person-years.
