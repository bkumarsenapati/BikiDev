b. Mean, Median, Mode
• Mean:
Add them up:
251+251+253+262+264+264+265+276+283+287+288+292+292+309+312
313+317+321+324+336 = 5760
Divide by 20 (since 20 flights)
5760 20 = $288.00

• Median (The middle number. Think of a median of a highway. It's in the "middle" of the road.)
Eliminate until you get a median (middle) or two numbers in the middle
251+251+253+262+264+264+265+276+283+287+288+292+292+309+312
313+317+321+324+336
After eliminating: = 287, 288
Add them together = 575
Divide by 2 =$287.50
Remember if you have two middle numbers, you must find their average to get the median.

• Mode- the number that appears the most (mode rhymes with most).
