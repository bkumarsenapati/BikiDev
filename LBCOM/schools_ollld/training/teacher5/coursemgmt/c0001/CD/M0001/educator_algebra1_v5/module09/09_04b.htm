  1. Solve and graph the inequalities below. There are several options for submitting your graphs. Please choose one of the options below:
    • Draw the graphs by hand and submit them by fax or mail to your instructor.
    • Create the graphs in a draw program or using the whiteboard feature and attach them
    • Describe your graphs using words. (See sample below)

Here is a sample "word" description:
The solution is x < -2 and x > 5. There would be a open circle on the 5 which shades to the right and an open circle on the -2 to the left. Since this is an and statement, there are no solutions. The two graphs will not intersect.

  1. After completing the problems below, submit your inequality answers and your graphs as your 09.04 Honors assessment.

    1. 2x > -4 and x - 4 < 2

    2. x + 3 > 2x + 1 and -4x < -8

    3. -6 < x + 3 > 6 (Remember this is an "and" statement)

    4. -3x > -6 or x - 5 < 2

    5. x + 2 > 2x - 1 or -6 > -2x - 2
