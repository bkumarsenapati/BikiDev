Your turn: Stem and Leaf chart

The price to fly from Orlando to New York City varies depending on the airline that you choose and the time and date of the trip. The stem and leaf chart contains 20 prices found. Some flights had the same price; for instance, on the first line you can see that there are two different flights for $251. These are both valid data points.

Airline Prices in $

Your turn: Tree Diagram

Eleanor thinks a different pair of shoes means an entirely different outfit.

Eleanor's outfits
