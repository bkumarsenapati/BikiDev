Beware of the following situations in which you could be kicked out of the test:

  • If you are not working constantly in the test, your ISP may kick you off-line. You cannot open the test and then walk away from the computer.
  • You cannot minimize your browser during the test.
  • You cannot move around in other parts of the course while the test is open.
  • You cannot work in more than one browser at a time while in the test.
  • You cannot enter the test, print it and then cancel out. Canceling out still counts as one submission and sends the test without any answers.
