The Language of Limits

In this lesson you will learn what applications involve limits.

Lesson Objective

After completing this lesson, you will be able to understand various applications of limits, such as instantaneous and average rate of change, as well as motion of an object or particle: displacement, distance, and velocity.


To meet this objective you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Instantaneous and Average Rates of Change
  • Distance, Displacement, and Velocity
  • Examples of Average and Instant Rates of Change
