Lessons & Assessments: Submitting Your Work

Submitting your work

As you complete each lesson, you typically will need to submit an assignment or an exam for grading.

Rubrics

Some assignments in this course contain a grading rubric that your teacher will use to grade your work. The individual rubrics can be found within the Discussion-Based Assessment or Collaborative Lessons.

Rubrics show you exactly the kind of work your teacher will expect from you. Before you submit your assignment, compare it against the rubric to make sure you're turning in the best work possible.
