02.06 Percentages
Throughout your years in school you have been taught to work percent problems based on three different types. These three types were worded in one of the following ways:

What is 56% of 38?

56 is what percent of 112?

56 is 25% of what number?  
The next thing you were asked to do is divide. Divide what by what? How can I tell and do I really care? Your worries are over. All you have to remember is a simple proportion and all your troubles are over. At least your problems with percent are over! Here's that little proportion you've been waiting for...
Remember - these are just shortcuts for getting answers.

Let's take a look at the first problem and see how this proportion will help us.

First let's set up the proportion with the items that we know will always have to be there. In order to solve any proportion, we must know three of the four parts and the remaining part is our unknown or what we're trying to solve for.
