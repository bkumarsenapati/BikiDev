Do you know how to...?

  • Write an equation of a line in slope intercept form. Find the slope of a line when given two points. Identify the equations and slope of horizontal and vertical lines. Graph a line using the slope intercept methodWrite an equation of a line in standard form when given a parallel line and a given point.
  • Solve a system of equations using both the Addition and Substitution methods.

Here are some review problems for each skill listed above. The answers can be found at the bottom of this section.

  1. Write the equation 2x - 3y = 6 in slope intercept form.
  2. Find the slope of the line that goes through the point (6,-1) and (-5,3)
  3. Write an equation of a horizontal line going through the point (-3,2) and give the slope of the line.
  4. Write an equation of a vertical line going through the point (-1,4) and give the slope of the line.
  5. Use the slope and the y-intercept to graph the line y = 3/4x - 1 on paper and then write a description of the steps.
  6. Write an equation of a line that is parallel to x - 3y = -21 and passing through the point (-6,1)
  7. Solve the system of equations using the substitution method: 3x + 2y = 1 and x + y = 4
  8. Solve the system of equations using the addition method: 4x - y = 6 and 3x + 2y = -1
