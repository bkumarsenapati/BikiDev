Systems of Equations

To solve by substitution:

Step 1: Solve for one of the variables in terms of the other.


Step 2: Substitute your expression for that variable in the other equation.


Step 3: Solve for the variable.


Step 4: Substitute your value for that variable in either original equation.


Step 5: Solve for the remaining variable. 		To solve by addition and multiplication:

Step 1: Get the same coefficient (opposite sign) for one of the variables in each equation.


Step 2: Add the equations to eliminate that variable.


Step 3: Solve for the variable.


Step 4: Substitute your value for that variable in either original equation.


Step 5: Solve for the remaining variable


Solving Systems of Two Equation By Substitution

Solve:

2x + y = 11
4x - 3y = 7

Step 1: Solve the first equation for y: y = 11 - 2x

Step 2: Substitute 11 - 2x for y in the second equation

4x - 3(11-2x) = 7

Step 3: Solve the last equation for x:

4x -33 + 6x = 7
10x = 40
x = 4

Step 4: Substitute 4 for x in the first equation

2(4) + y = 11

Step 5: Solve it for y.

8 + y = 11

y = 3

Always solve for both variables.
The solution is the ordered pair (4,3).

Check:

2x + y = 2 x 4 + 3 = 8 + 3 = 11
4x - 3y = 4 x 4 - 3 x 3 = 16 - 9 = 7
The solution is the ordered pair (4,3)

Solving Systems of Equation by Multiplication and Addition

Solve:

2x - 3y = 15
3x - 5y = 10

Step 1: Multiply each equation by a number that will allow you to eliminate one variable with addition. To do this you must get similar coefficients with opposite signs.

3(2x -3y = 15)
-2(3x -5y =10)

Step 2: Add the equations.

6x -9y = 45
-6x + 10y = -20
y = 25

Step 3: Solve for the variable. (done here because the coefficient is 1.)

Step 4: Substitute the value of y back into either original equation to solve for x.

2x - 3(25) = 15

Step 5: Solve for x.

2x - 75 = 15
2x = 90
x = 45

Answer: (45,25)

Always solve for both variables.
The solution is the ordered pair (45, 25).

Check (both equations)

2(45) - 3(25) = 15 3(45) - 5(25) = 10
90 - 75 = 15 135 -125 = 10
15 = 15 10 = 10

The correct answer is (45,25).
