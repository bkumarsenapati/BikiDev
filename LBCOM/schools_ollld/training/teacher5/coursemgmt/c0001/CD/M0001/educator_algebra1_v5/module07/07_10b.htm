07.10 HONORS Lab Assignment

Assignment 07.10 HONORS Lab Assignment

  1. Read and follow the directions for both parts of the lab.
  2. Include the following items when you submit "07.10 HONORS Lab" to your instructor. Be organized with your work so the instructor can easily grade it and give you feedback.

Lab Part 1

    1. Give the measured length, width and diagonal of your cement block.
    2. Give the diagonal of your cement block that you found using the Pythagorean Theorem.
    3. Show your work with the distance formula for sides BC and AC ONLY.
    4. Answer the 3 questions from this part of the lab.

Lab Part 2

    1. Show your work with the equation and the quadratic formula.
    2. Answer the four questions from this part of the lab.
    3. Use the lab report template to write your lab report and submit your report for "07.10 HONORS Lab" to your instructor.

This assignment is worth 20 points.
