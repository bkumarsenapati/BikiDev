Pace Options

The Individual Pace Planner is a template that allows the teacher or student to input information to formulate a completion plan for the course. This can be based on planned hours in the course per week or start and end dates. This planner will provide a PDF that outlines what assessments should be completed each week to accomplish the goals set based on the information provided.
