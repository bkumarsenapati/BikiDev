03.08 Connections Assignment

Please answer each of the following questions below. Record your answers on a sheet of paper to reference while completing 03.08 Connections.

  1. Joe is 5 years older than Sally. Represent Joe's age in terms of Sally's age. Use "S" for Sally's age.
  2. The length of a rectangle is 3 more than twice its width. The perimeter is 48 feet.
  3. Fifteen more than four times a number is 6 more than five times the number. Use "n" for the number.
  4. Martha and Sally had lunch together on Tuesday. Sally paid $2 more than Martha. The total bill was $5.64.
  5. Bob paid $3 less than Tim at a local pizza parlor. Together they spent $7.80.
  6. There were six more women on a committee than men. The committee was made up of 30 people.
  7. Three consecutive integers have the sum of 153.
  8. Five times the first of three consecutive odd integers is fourteen more than three times the second.
  9. The sum of three consecutive even integers is fifty-four.
  10. An airplane flies at a rate of 356 km/h . The plane travels a total distance of 1424 km.
    (Use Distance = Rate * Time for your equation.)
