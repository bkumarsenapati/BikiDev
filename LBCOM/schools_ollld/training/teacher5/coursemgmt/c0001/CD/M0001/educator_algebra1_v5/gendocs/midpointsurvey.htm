Midpoint Survey

Please complete this activity after you have finished Module 5 and the Semester Exam.

The administration prepared an online survey for you to fill out. The purpose of this survey is for us to gather some information about our students so that we can continually make your online learning experience a successful one.

Your instructor will have access to general statistics created by the survey but will not have access to your personal responses to the survey questions. Your answers will be very helpful in giving feedback to your instructor and your input is very important to them.

Please complete this survey even though you may have already done so in another online course. Some of the survey questions are unique to this course.

Please access and complete the survey.
