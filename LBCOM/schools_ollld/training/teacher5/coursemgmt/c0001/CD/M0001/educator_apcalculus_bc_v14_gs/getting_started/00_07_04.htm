Academic Integrity (continued)

How do we monitor integrity?

On-line identification tools are used to compare work submitted to the internet as well as to all prior assessments submitted by all students.

Discussion-Based Assessments are conducted routinely between the instructor and the student. These must be completed free of any distraction or influence from others. This is also a time to ask your instructor questions.

Proctored exams are a routine part of how we ensure student learning is taking place. These include random selection of students, as well. Refusal to take a proctored exam at any time will result in a failing grade in the course.
