01.06 Effective Learning Resources

Let's face it! Math is tough. Learning math online can be a challenging experience. How can you face the challenge and be successful?

My answer is, "Know your resources and do not be afraid to use them." Following is a list of valuable resources.

  • Your instructor
    Your instructor is your number one resource. Communicate, ask questions, turn in work, read your instructor's comments. They are here to support you in your learning.
  • Web sites
    The following web sites are great for math games, examples, information and illustrations. Save them in your favorites and become a self-learner by visiting them often.

  • The whiteboard and chat room.
    Your instructor will most likely hold chats throughout the week. Be sure to go to them. This is an opportunity to interact with your teacher as you would in a face-to-face environment.
