Making Connections

Much of math, engineering, and physics rely on being able to solve differential equations. In this lesson you will learn how to connect one or more derivatives of a function with the function itself. You will also learn that function behavior depends upon the constant, but then you probably already guessed that! Right?

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • evaluate constants of integration given an initial condition
  • solve separable differential equations
  • model various applications using separable differential equations, with particular focus on the study of the equation y ′ = ky and exponential growth

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Differential Equations—The Basic Idea
  • Separation of Variables
  • Separation of Variables Continued
  • Exponential Growth and Decay
