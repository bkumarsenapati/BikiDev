05.00 Pretest 

 

Your results on the pretest will be used to determine what you already know about the concepts taught in this module. Please do not use any notes or reference materials.  If you do not really know the material, it is to your benefit to do all the lessons.

If you do not know the material on the pretest, skip it and complete all the assignments in the module.

After you submit the pretest, follow the step by step chart until you hear from your instructor. Your teacher will grade your pretest and let you know if you are able to skip any assignments in Module 5.

You will only be allowed to skip assignments in which you show mastery of the concepts. Your teacher will be the only person involved in deciding which assignments, if any, should be skipped.
