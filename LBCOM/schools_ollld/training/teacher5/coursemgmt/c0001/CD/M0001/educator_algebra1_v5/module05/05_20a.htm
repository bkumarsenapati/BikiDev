05.20 Semester Exam Introduction

Step 1: Be Prepared!

  • Please review all the information taught in this module before you take the test.
  • Be sure to visit the Semester Exam Review Page beginning with Module 2.
  • If you have any questions or concerns about the material, contact your instructor.

You will only be allowed to take this test one time. Once you enter the test, it will count as your submission. Here is a list of several situations in which you could be kicked out of the test thus using your only submission.

  • If you are not working constantly in the test then your ISP may kick you off-line. You cannot open the test and then walk away from the computer.
  • You cannot minimize your browser during the test.
  • You cannot move around in other parts of the course while the test is open.
  • You cannot work in more than one browser at a time while in the test.
  • You cannot enter the test, print it and then cancel out. Canceling out will still count as one submission and will send the test without any answers.
