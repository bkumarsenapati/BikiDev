Enter the third variable! Many great curves cannot be written down as a single equation. In this lesson you will learn how to work with parametric equations!

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • analyze planar curves given in parametric form
  • compute derivatives of parametric functions and their applications such as finding tangent lines
  • find the arc length of a curve described in Cartesian form as well as parametric form
  • convert between parametric form and other forms (such as rectangular/Cartesian form)
  • graph curves in parametric form, including using technology to do so

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Introduction to Polar Curves
  • Introduction to Parametric Curves
  • More Examples of Parametric Curves
  • Derivatives of Parametric Curves
  • More Derivatives of Parametric Curves
