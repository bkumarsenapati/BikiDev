Getting Started Module—Installation Instructions

(release v1.1 7.29.13)

Follow these instructions on how to add the Getting Started module to your course and customize it.

1. Get the files.

Copy the Collaborate/Develop/getting_started folder into the root level of your course.

2. Update the sitemap.

Update your course sitemap.xml file by adding the code from getting_started_sitemap_code.xml. Make sure to copy from the source code, as there are escaped characters in the xml.

3. Customize the content.

Customize the content on the following pages:

  • 00_02_01.htm - (Navigation) You will need to replace the screenshot in the interactive with a screenshot of your course. This means you may also need to adjust/add/remove hotspots to match the course navigation.
  • 00_03_01.htm - (Lessons & Assessments) You may want to change the image to match your course look and feel.
  • 00_04 all pages - (Course Specifics) This entire lesson is custom to your course and needs to be modified accordingly.

Optional:

You may want to change other images to better match your course. That is at your discretion.

4. Customize CSS.

Change any coloring/styling in the file: getting_started/css/getting_started.css

5. Remove extra files.

Remove the getting_started_sitemap_code.xml and readhme.htm files from your copy of the Getting Started module.

You're done!
