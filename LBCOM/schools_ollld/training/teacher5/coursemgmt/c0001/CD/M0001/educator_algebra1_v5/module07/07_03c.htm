Assignment 07.03 Factoring by Grouping

  1. In your own words, explain the factoring by grouping process. Write it in your notebook.
  2. Read the examples in the lesson and work out the practice problems on your own paper. Be sure to check your answers.
  3. Work through the following problems on your own paper or in a word document. Submit your work and answers to your instructor for Assignment 07.03 Factor by Grouping. To write your exponents, you may indicate exponents with the ^ symbol located above the number 6 on your keyboard. For example, x4 would be written x^4.
    1. ac - bc + ad - bd
    2. xy - 3x - 8y + 24
    3. 4x2 + 20x + 5y +xy
    4. 48x2y + 20xz +12xy + 5z

This assignment is worth 10 points.
