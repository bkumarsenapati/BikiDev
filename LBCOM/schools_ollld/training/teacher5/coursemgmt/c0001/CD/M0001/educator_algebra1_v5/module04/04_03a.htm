04.03 Solving Equations

This is what algebra is all about: Solving Equations!

Linear Equations
In general, when solving equations you are trying to get the variable alone on one side of the equal sign. You do this by performing the opposite math operation. Opposite of addition is subtraction and vice versa. Opposite of multiplication is division and vice versa.

You also want to keep in mind that whatever operation is performed on one side of the equation must also be performed on the other side of the equation. We will call this "keeping the equation balanced."

Travel Advisory:

  • Solving equations is one of the most important skills in Algebra.
  • In order to move successfully through the course, you must know how to solve equations of all types.

Multi-Step Equations

Step 1: Distribute on each side of the equation.
Step 2: Collect like terms on each side of the equation.
Step 3: Solve for the variable.
