Extreme means situated at the farthest possible point from a center. In this lesson you will learn extrema of the function refers to the minimums and maximums.

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • determine the intervals for where a function is increasing or decreasing; analytically, numerically, and with a graphing calculator
  • sketch the curve of a function based upon information from its first and second derivatives and vice versa
  • determine the global or absolute extrema of a function on a closed interval, using both algebraic analytical techniques with the 1st, 2nd, or both derivatives as well as with the use of a graphing calculator

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Extrema Local and Absolute Maximums and Minimums
  • Practice Questions on Extrema
  • More Practice Questions Involving Extrema
