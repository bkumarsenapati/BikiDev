Honors Algebra 1

If you would like to complete the honors portion of Algebra 1, please contact your instructor by phone or email at the end of Module 3.

There are both honors assignments and honors tests to complete throughout the course. These assignments and assessments are in addition to all the work in the regular course. The workload can be more intense and the concepts are challenging. If you are an honors student, you should be able to work through most of these assignments independently or by asking very specific questions. If you are finding that you need step by step voice instructions about the honors assignments then you should probably reconsider your decision to complete the honors parts of the course.
In order to receive honors credit for the course, you MUST complete the course in 9 months or less. Students working in Algebra 1 honors will NOT be allowed to work on the extended pace.
