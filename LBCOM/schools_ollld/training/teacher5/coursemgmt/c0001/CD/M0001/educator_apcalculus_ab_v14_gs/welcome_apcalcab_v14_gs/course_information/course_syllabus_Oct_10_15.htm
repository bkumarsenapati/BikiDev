Course Syllabus

Description:

Comparable to college and university calculus, this course will help prepare you for the Calculus AB Advanced Placement exam. This course meets one required math credit for high school graduation.

Estimated Completion Time: 2 segments / 32-36 weeks

Course Assessment and Participation Requirements:

To achieve success, students are expected to submit work in each course weekly. Students can learn at their own pace; however, “any pace” still means that students must make progress in the course every week. To measure learning, students complete self-checks, practice lessons, multiple choice questions, projects, discussion-based assessments, and discussions. Students are expected to maintain regular contact with teachers; the minimum requirement is monthly. When teachers, students, and parents work together, students are successful.
