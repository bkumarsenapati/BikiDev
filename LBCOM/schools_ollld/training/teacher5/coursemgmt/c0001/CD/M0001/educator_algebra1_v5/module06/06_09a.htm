06.09 Addition and Subtraction of Radicals
Addition and subtraction of radical expressions are similar to those on polynomials ...complex problems must be simplified prior to the operation, and sometimes afterward. Even though you might not see a need to simplify, remember the focus! Complex equations in math and science need to be simplified! Formulas in the workplace need to meet the same standards!
