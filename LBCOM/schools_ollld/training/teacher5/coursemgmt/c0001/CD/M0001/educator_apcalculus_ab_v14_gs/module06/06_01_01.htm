Mapping Mountains

In this lesson you will meet one of the leading mathematicians of the nineteenth century, use his sum to simplify your notation, and find the area under and between curves. Super cool!

Lesson Objective

After completing this lesson, you will be able to use the method of discs/slicing/washers to find the volume of a solid revolution.


To meet this objective you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Bernhard Riemann—Founder of Riemann Sums
  • Area Under a Curve
  • Area Between Two Curves
  • Area Between Two Curves, Cont.
