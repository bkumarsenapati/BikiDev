Warning: Vectors Are Wild

In this lesson you will discover what represents quantities of magnitude and a direction and play around with n-dimensional space!

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • analyze planar curves given in vector form
  • compute derivatives of vector functions, including their application in rectilinear motion and calculating related properties such as distance, displacement, velocity, acceleration, jerk, etc.

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Vectors
  • Vectors Continued
  • Vectors and Motion
