10.02 Review

The links below will take you to lessons for review of Modules 6, 7, and 8. If you need more examples you should return to the lessons for more details. Your Module quizzes, tests and pre-tests are another good source for problems. In most cases, you will have the answers to the ones you missed making it easy for you to use them as a study guide.

The Module review is worth 50 points. When you are done with the work, be sure to check your answers from the link below. Contact your instructor if you have questions or concerns about any of the concepts.

Submit a summary of your progress your 10.02 Review assessment.

  • You do not need to to submit all the work.
  • Simply write 2-3 sentences telling your instructor how you did on the work and including any questions you may still want to ask.

  • Click on each topic and you will go to a review page for the concept listed.
  • Read the review and work the given problems to prepare for your final exam.
Operations on Polynomials Review multiplying polynomials. This review includes FOIL, distributing and collecting like terms. There are nine practice problems included.
Operations on Radicals Review operations on radicals. This review includes simplification, multiplication, division and rationalizing the denominator. Nine practice problems are included.
Pythagorean Theorem Review the Pythagorean Theorem. There are two practice problems. Please follow directions. Don't forget the units.
Factoring This is a review of factoring by grouping. This is the only method you need to really know. You may use the shortcuts, but several of these problems cannot be factored by the shortcuts. There are eight problems.
Solving Quadratic Equations There are three methods of solving quadratic equations. Click here for a review of three of them and 8 practice problems.
Simplifying, multiplying and dividing Rational Expressions This is a review of simplifying, multiplying and dividing rational expressions. There are five review problems.
Addition and subtraction of Rational Expressions This is a review of adding and subtracting rational expressions with like denominators. Pay special attention to subtraction. The negative sign must be distributed. There are three practice problems.
Solving Equations with Rational Expressions This is a review of solving equations with rational expressions. There are three practice problems.
