Slopes of Horizontal Lines

Summary facts about horizontal lines

  • A horizontal line has a slope of zero.
  • A horizontal line is drawn left to right like the horizon.
  • The equation of a horizontal line is y = some number.
  • All points on a horizontal line will have the same y value.

Summary facts about vertical lines

  • A vertical line has an undefined slope.
  • A vertical line is drawn up and down.
  • The equation of a vertical line is x = some number.
  • All points on a vertical line will have the same x value.

What about their slopes?

The slope of each line is 2. We know that because

the line is in Slope Intercept form (y = mx+b)

and the m value is 2.

The slopes are the same.

Therefore: Parallel lines have the same slope.
