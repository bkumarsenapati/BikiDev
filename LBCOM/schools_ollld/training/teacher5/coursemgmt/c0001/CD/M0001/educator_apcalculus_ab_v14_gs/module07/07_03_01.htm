Not all integrals can be computed. HUH? That said, you can estimate values of definite integrals and this lesson will show you how!

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • use numerical approximation techniques to evaluate definite integrals, where appropriate, including the area under a curve
  • use Riemann Sums (using left, right, and midpoint evaluation points) and trapezoidal sums to approximate definite integrals of functions through algebraic, graphical, and tabular representation (table of values)
  • discuss error implications of different methods
