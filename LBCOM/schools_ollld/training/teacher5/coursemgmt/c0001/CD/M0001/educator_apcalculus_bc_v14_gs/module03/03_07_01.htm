Not Everything Is As It Seems …

Not all functions will fall into an easy form to find the derivative. In this lesson you will learn how to use implicit differentiation to find the derivative and use logarithmic differentiation as well!

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • use implicit differentiation to find the derivative/slope of a curve that is defined implicitly
  • use logarithmic differentiation to find derivatives

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Implicit Differentiation—Introduction Examples
  • Implicit Differentiation—Logarithmic Differentiation
