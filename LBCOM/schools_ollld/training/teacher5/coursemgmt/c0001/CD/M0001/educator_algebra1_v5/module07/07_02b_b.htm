07.02 Difference of Squares Practice Solutions

  1. (a + 3)(a - 3) OR (a - 3)(a + 3)
    Remember the order of the factors is not important.

  2. Prime
    This expression is prime because it is an addition problem. It is not a "difference" of squares.

  3. (3p3 + 5)(3p3 - 5)

  4. 8(x2 - 2)
    Tricky! This is as far as you can go because 2 is not a perfect square.
  5. Prime
    This expression is prime because of the power of 7 on the x. The powers have to be even in order to split them evenly.

  6. 2(4x6 - 9)
    2(2x3 + 3)(2x3 - 3) is the final answer.

  7. 2(4p14 - 25q4)
    2(2p7 + 5q2)(2p7 - 5q2) is the final answer.

  8. (2a2 + b)(2a2 - b)
  1. (a + 5)(a - 5)

  2. (x + 4y)(x - 4y)

  3. (p3 + 7)(p3 - 7)

  4. x(25x2 - 16)
    x(5x + 4)(5x - 4) is the final answer.

  5. (x + y)(x - y)

  6. (2 + 3y2)(2 - 3y2)

  7. (p2 + 4)(p2 - 4)
    (p2 + 4)(p + 2)(p - 2) is the final answer.

  8. a(a4 - 1)
    a(a2 + 1)(a2 - 1)
    a(a2 + 1)(a + 1)(a - 1) is the final answer.
