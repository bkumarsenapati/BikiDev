04.04 Advanced Equations Practice Answers


1. 2(x - 3) + 10 = -4(x + 6)

answer: -14/3

Distribute first:
2x - 6 + 10 = -4x - 24

Combine like terms on each side:
2x + 4 = -4x - 24

Solve for x:
2x + 4 = -4x - 24
+4x +4x (add 4x to both sides)
6x + 4 = -24
-4 -4 (subtract 4 from both sides)
6x = -28
/6 /6 (divide both sides by 6)
x = -28/6 = -14/3 (you can have fractions as answers)

2. 5(x + 2) = 5x + 10

answer: All Real Numbers

Distribute first:
5x + 10 = 5x + 10
-5x -5x (subtract 5x from both sides)
10 = 10 is always true so the answer is "All Real Numbers"

3. 8x - 4 = 2(4x + 5)

answer: No Solutions

Distribute first:
8x - 4 = 8x + 10
-8x -8x (subtract 8x from both sides)
-4 = 10 is never true so the answer is "No Solutions"
