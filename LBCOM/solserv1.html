Hotschools Learning System

Hotschools.net is a comprehensive eLearning solution for institutions to implement innovative distance and web-enhanced education solutions. The industry’s easiest platform for teaching and learning delivers on the growing power of the Internet without straining school budgets for new equipment and training. The Hotschools.net solution suite utilizes open architecture for reliability, scalability, and ease of adoption. Our solution is designed for ease of integration with existing systems in the schools.  The Hotschools.net solution brings the classroom, content, grades, and self-assessment tools seamlessly together for a unique teaching and learning experience.

 

Learner Perspective:

It is very easy to use and apart from the anytime-anywhere learning benefits of a web-based learning environment the learners will be able to take advantage of collaborative learning. It provides the opportunity for wider collaboration with peers and educators across the globe. The formative self-assessment with immediate feedback helps learner in ensuring steady progress. The grade-book allows learner/parent to monitor progress.

Learner tools/services:

Online Assessment Services – Learners can take online exams created by their educators. The test scores are automatically posted to the Grade Book and are available to the learner, educators, and the administrator as well.

Course Services – Learners can register for courses and take those courses online. Learners can log in to take their courses online, check for homework assignments, complete their homework and upload it back for evaluation by the educators.

Performance Evaluation – Learners can access their test performances and review the test scores using scores from multiple exams. Options are available to export performance data in various standard data formats for graphical presentations.

Chat  – Learners can join chat rooms for collaborating with peers, educators or subject matter expert.

Forums  – To share information about curricular/extra-curricular activities

Organizer  – To organize learner appointments, reminders, class schedule, and meetings.

 

Educator Perspective:

Educators can provide one-on-one teaching/tutoring to their learners. Hotschools.net Courseware Manager allows creating, publishing, and scheduling media rich content to meet the learning needs of learners. All kinds of files (doc, ppt, flash movie, audio/video) created using any authoring tool can be included in the course material hosted on, and served by a Hotschools.net server. Creating and scheduling password protected exams, quizzes, and surveys are very easy via intuitive user interfaces. Some question types (multiple choice, multiple answer, fill in the blanks, yes/no, matching, and ordering type) are evaluated immediately, while the essay type questions are queued. Teacher can evaluate them online and submit scores. Grade-book updating is automatic after all submissions are evaluated. The whole cycle of composing an exam, scheduling and delivering it to learners, answer submission by learners, evaluation and grade reporting is automatic making it very easy for a teacher to handle even a large class. Content or question reusability makes it easy to tailor existing material for current learning context. Grade book with multiple views allows convenient monitoring of learner/class performance. An educator can hold virtual classes/tutorials with the help of available synchronous tools, and also can use asynchronous tools to post offline messages or emails.

Educator Tools/Services:

Online Courseware Manager – Educators can create, upload, and make their custom courses available on the Internet, using wizards that are self-explanatory.

Online Assessment Manager – permits Educators to create their own Assessments (exams, quizzes, tests, surveys etc.) and schedule them online to their learners using the Web.

Online Question Editor – Educators can create questions (Test Items) and add them to their Question Banks using a Web based interface.

Performance Evaluation – Educators can evaluate learner and the assess the overall class performance by processing the electronic grade books. The learner-performance data can be exported to various standard data formats and can be represented graphically.

Chat – Permits synchronous communication between educators/subject matter experts and their learners. Allows one to join, create public or private chat rooms, and use shared whiteboard.

Virtual Class Services – allows an Educator to establish and conduct audio and video based, real time virtual classes with an individual learner, or a small group of learners.

Forums – to post various topics, including items like homework assignments, online tutoring schedules, school events or teacher announcements.

Notice Boards – The Administrator creates the Notice Boards and posts the Messages.

Organizer – For managing appointments, reminders and meeting-schedules.

 

Administrator perspective:

Most of the "administrative" tasks like creating/scheduling courses/classes/exams etc. can be performed by teachers themselves. The current administration module covers creating colleges/universities/departments, teachers, and learners. This module will be extended to incorporate customized workflow and policy/rules. This administration module is not intended for the general administration of an organization.

Administrator tools:

Administrative e-Collaboration (Web Based File and Document Management tools) - allow the administrative staff and educators to collaborate (on lesson plans, course outlines, presentations, for example), share, compose, and review documents online.

Performance Evaluation – to monitor learner and class performance by means of different views of grade books.

Forums – to post various communications, reminders, school events, topical messages or policies.

Organizer – organizes calendar, appointments, reminders and scheduled meetings.

Virtual School Solutions

Hotschools.net has created a revolutionary eLearning platform for virtual school creation. Like the Practice Center, the entire platform is web based, requiring no special hardware or software, only access to the Internet, utilizing a standard browser, such as Internet Explorer.The platform can be hosted by Hotschools.net and provided as an ASP (application service provider) offering, or licensed to individual school districts. The platform offers several revolutionary features.

The power of the Internet can now be harnessed to allow teachers and administrators in the next room or on the other side of the world to collaborate on courses, outlines, teaching methodologies, and share opinions, attitudes, and information.

A teacher friendly proprietary software technology of Hotschools.net useful for a teacher to easily Post, Edit and View grades of students. Teacher can view the performance categories of the student and access the list of students under each category.

Online Assessment is the function of the Grade Manager. The Grade Manager displays the list of students who had submitted a relevant performance category for assessment. Teacher can Add, Edit and View the corresponding score and grade of the student for a particular course.
