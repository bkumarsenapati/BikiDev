Creating a folder on your computer
It is recommended that you print this page for future reference.

Illustrated below is one way to create an Algebra folder in which you will save all of your work for this course.

1. Go to your desktop and open "My Computer."

2. Open the view of your (C:) drive.

You will see all the files, folders, and programs that are housed in your (C:) drive.

3. To make a new folder, choose File, New, Folder.

A new folder will appear in your (C:) drive window. It is probably at the bottom and you will have to scroll down to find it.

4. To change the name of the folder, right click on it and choose Rename. Be sure to RIGHT click.

5. Rename the folder. Name it Algebra. You will save all your work in this folder. Remember that it is located in the (C:) drive.
