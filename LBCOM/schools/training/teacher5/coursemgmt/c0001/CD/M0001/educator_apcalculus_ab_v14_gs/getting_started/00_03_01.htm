Lessons & Assessments

Parts of a lesson

Beginning: Each lesson begins with an introduction and objectives. These will give you a preview of the concepts you will be learning and will be expected to understand.

Middle: Read each lesson carefully to understand all of the concepts. The lesson section may contain several pages or screens. Also, be sure to complete the activities. You will practice the concepts and learn from your mistakes, so you can be better prepared before test time.

End: Each lesson ends with information about the assignment or exam you will need to turn in for a grade and directions for how to submit this work.
