Put on your roller blades, get out your skateboard, or take out your favorite toy race car (sneakers will work, too), and grab a friend, because you need to move swiftly in order to start Module 4 Racing Lab.

    3. Tape

    4. Stopwatch

    5. Measuring tape

    6. Parent/Guardian
