More Curves Ahead!

In this lesson you will work with polar curves! Get ready!

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • analyze planar curves given in polar form
  • compute derivatives of polar functions and their applications such as finding tangent lines
  • find the area between polar curves
  • convert between polar form and other forms (such as rectangular/Cartesian form)
  • graph curves in polar form, including using technology to do so

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Polar Coordinates
  • Converting Between Cartesian and Polar Coordinates
  • Derivatives of Polar Curves
