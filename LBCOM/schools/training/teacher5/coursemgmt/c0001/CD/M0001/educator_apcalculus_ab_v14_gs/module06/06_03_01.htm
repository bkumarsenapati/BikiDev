Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • explore and understand the Mean Value Theorem for Integrals
  • find the average value of a function
  • explore Rectilinear Motion with Integrals
  • explore General Motion of Objects (distance, displacement, velocity, speed, and acceleration)
  • evaluate constants of integration given an initial condition

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • The Mean Value Theorem for Integrals
  • Position, Velocity and Acceleration, with Integrals
  • Distance and Displacement with Integrals
