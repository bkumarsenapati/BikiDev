Feel the Power!

With great power comes even greater responsibility. In this lesson you will learn how to apply polynomial series … use this knowledge wisely!

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • use polynomial series to approximate irrational numbers
  • use polynomial series to solve differential equations
  • use polynomial series to Do rectilinear motion
  • use polynomial series to model real-life behavior
  • use polynomial series to evaluate limits

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Using Polynomial Series to Solve Differential Equations
  • Using Polynomial Series to Approximate Irrational Numbers
  • Using Polynomial Series to Model Real-Life Behavior
  • Using Polynomial Series to Model Real-Life Behavior Part 2
  • Using Polynomial Series to Model Real-Life Behavior Part 3
