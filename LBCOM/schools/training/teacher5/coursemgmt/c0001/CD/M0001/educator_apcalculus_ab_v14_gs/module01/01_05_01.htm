Be Creative!

In this lesson you will learn how to create and experiment with function composition.

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • perform combinations of functions arithmetically or through composition to create new functions
  • perform translations, reflections, and expansions/compressions on functions
  • use technology such as a graphing calculator to experiment with functions

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Compositions
  • Vertical Stretches and Reflections
  • Horizontal Stretches and Reflections
  • Vertical and Horizontal Translations
  • Even and Odd Functions
