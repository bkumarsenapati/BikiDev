Are They Related? They Look Alike.

In this lesson you will learn how to solve related rates using Calculus!

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • use derivatives to solve related rate problems
  • model how the rates of different quantities that depend upon the same parameter, such as time, interact

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • The Basic Idea and a Trigonometry Question
  • A Pythagoras Question
  • A Similar Triangles Question
