Mathematical Back-flips …

In this lesson you will learn one of the most important theorems in mathematical analysis! You will also learn how to use a derivative to help evaluate limits involving indeterminate forms … and how to do a back-flip … just kidding.

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • use the Mean Value Theorem for Derivatives to make conclusion about a function on certain intervals (and point within those intervals) and explore the results via graphical methods
  • evaluate limits involving indeterminate forms using L’Hôpital’s Rule

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • The Mean-Value Theorem for Derivatives
  • L’Hôpital’s Rule
