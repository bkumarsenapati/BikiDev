Absolutely Simple

In this lesson you will learn the three ways convergence of the power series can occur. You will probably need to use a test or two … so go wrestle those notes from the dog!

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • calculate the interval and radius of convergence of a given power series
  • be familiar with the interval of convergence for the common power series (Maclaurin series) for ex, sinx, cosx,

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Radius and Interval of Convergence of Power Series
  • More Examples of Power Series
  • Some More Examples of Power Series
  • Even More Examples of Power Series
