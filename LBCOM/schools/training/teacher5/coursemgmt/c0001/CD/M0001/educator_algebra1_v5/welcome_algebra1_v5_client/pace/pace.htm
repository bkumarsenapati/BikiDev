Pace

Several Pace chart options are provided below. The traditional pace is recommended for your class.

Traditional Pace Chart

Traditional Pace Chart
This is the pace suggested for most students it will allow you to complete the Algebra I course in a traditional school year (36 weeks).

Other Pace Charts

Accelerated Chart or 18-Week Block Schedule Chart
Students are working to complete the course in one semester. This chart is good for students working in a 4 by 4 or block schedule.

Extended Pace
Requires teacher, parent and guidance counselor approval. Your individual pace will be designed by your teacher. You will be required to submit a petition for extended pace documenting approval from your parents and your school guidance counselor.

2nd Semester Only Students Pace Chart
Only students who are completing the second half of this course will need to use this pace chart.
