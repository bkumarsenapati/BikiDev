04.03 Equations Answers

1. x + 4 = -9
Subtract 4 from each side: x + 4 - 4 = -9 - 4
x = -13

2. -2x = 6
Divide both sides by -2: -2x/-2 = 6/-2
x = -3

3. 3x + 5 = 17
Subtract 5 from each side: 3x + 5 - 5 = 17 - 5
3x = 12
Then divide each side by 3: 3x/3 = 12/3
x = 4

4. -5x = 15
Divide both sides by -5: -5x/-5 = 15/-5
x = -3

5. 4x - (2x + 6) = 20
4x - 2x - 6 = 20
2x - 6 = 20
Add 6 to both sides: 2x - 6 + 6 = 20 + 6
2x = 26
Divide both sides by 2: 2x/2 = 26/2
x = 13

6. 5 - (x - 4) = 10
5 - x + 4 = 10
9 - x = 10
Subtract 9 from both sides: 9 -9 -x = 10 - 9
-x = 1
Divide both sides by -1: -x/-1 = 1/-1
x = -1

7. 3(x - 2) - 2(x + 4) = 10
3x - 6 - 2x - 8 = 10
x - 14 = 10
Add 14 to both sides: x - 14 + 14 = 10 + 14
x = 24
