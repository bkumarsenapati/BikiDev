Convergent vs. Divergent

Not all areas on an infinite interval will yield infinite areas. In this lesson you will evaluate improper integrals and their applications. (Which includes using infinite intervals!)

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • identify integrals as improper and rewrite them as limits of definite integrals
  • determine whether an improper integral converges (to finite limit) or diverges
  • compute definite integrals that are improper in applications including the area under a curve or between curves and volume

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • The Basic Idea
  • Definitions and Examples
  • Some Important Examples
