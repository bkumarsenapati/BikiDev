Milk Chocolate Not Chocolate Milk

In this lesson you will learn that the derivative of a product is not the product of the derivatives … wait, what? No worries! This lesson shows you what that means!

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • develop derivatives of polynomial functions
  • use the product/quotient rules to find derivatives

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Basic Rules of Differentiation
  • The Product of Quotient Rules
  • Rules of Differentiation—Practice Questions
