02.06 Common Denominator Tips
There are many ways to find a common denominator and therefore many numbers that can be used as a common denominator of two fractions. The key to success is the awareness of various methods and finding a method that works consistently for you. Below you will find descriptions of various methods for finding a common denominator of two fractions. Be sure to contact your instructor if you have questions or need some extra practice with this skill.
