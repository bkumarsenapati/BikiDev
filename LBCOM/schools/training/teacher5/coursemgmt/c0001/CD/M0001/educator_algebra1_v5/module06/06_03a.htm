Putting it all together More complex problems may require that you use both the power to a power rule and the product rule from lesson 6.2.

Here is an example:


(2x5)2 x (-3x3)4

1. 4x10 x 81x12

Step 1: In the first term, multiply 2 times 2 and you get 4. Now use the shortcut and multiply the 5 times 2 to get x10.

In the second term multiply -3*-3*-3*-3. You get 81 as your answer. Now use your shortcut and multiply 3 times 4 to get x12.

2. 324x22

Step 2: To find the answer you will need to multiply 4 times 81 and then add the exponents of x.

***Remember if you're raising a power to a power you multiply the exponents. If you're multiplying, you add the exponents.
