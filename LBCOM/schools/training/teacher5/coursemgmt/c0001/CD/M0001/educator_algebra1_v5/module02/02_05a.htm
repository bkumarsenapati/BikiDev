02.05 Lab

"To be a square or a rectangle" - that is the question.


Background

Did you know that most people are either squares or rectangles? I know, you have been called many things (sweetie, cutie) but never a geometric shape. If the ratio of ones arm span to height is 1 to 1 or close to it, you are considered a square. If the ratio is not close to 1 to 1, you are a rectangle.

In 2.01, you created a data table that had the arm span measurements and heights of yourself and classmates. Get it out because we will be working with the data and adding to the table.

Before we begin the actual lab, you will need to do the following:
    1. Retrieve the data table file you created in 2.01 .
    2. Get a calculator (only if you want to use it to do calculations).
    3. Download the Algebra Lab Report in .rtf format. Note that when you click on this, you should save it into your Algebra folder that you created back in assignment 02.01.
Now you are ready to begin the lab. Click on the Exercises Tab at the top or the word Next at the bottom right to proceed.
