Assignment 04.02 Simplify
  1. Read the examples in the lesson and work out the practice problems on your own paper. The work from the practice problems is extremely important. Be sure to check your answers. If you get the practice problems wrong, you should contact your instructor before you move on to the assignment. These should go into your notebook.
  2. Simplify the 10 algebraic expressions below. You will use this information to complete assignment 04.02 Simplify.
    1. 4x + 6x - 8x
    2. -3x - 6x + x
    3. 2x + 3y - 4x
    4. (2x + 3y - 5) + (-3y - 6)
    5. (4x + 3y) - (2x -5y)
      (Hint: Remember to distribute the negative to the second set of parentheses)
    6. 2(3x - 1) + 15
    7. -3(2x - 1) - 5
    8. 3xy + 4xy - 6xz
    9. 7xy - 2xz + 4xz - 12xyz + 2zx
      (Hint: The "xz" terms and the "zx" terms are alike so they can be combined.)
    10. -3(2x - 5) - 4(x + 7)
