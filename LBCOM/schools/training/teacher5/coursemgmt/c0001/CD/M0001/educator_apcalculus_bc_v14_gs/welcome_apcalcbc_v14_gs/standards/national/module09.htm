National Standards: Module Nine

National Standards by Lesson
Module Nine Lessons National Standards
09.00 Module Nine Checklist and Pretest All Module Standards
09.01 Parametric Curves Analyze planar curves given in parametric form; be able to compute derivatives of parametric functions and their applications such as finding tangent lines; find the arc length of a curve described in Cartesian form as well as parametric form; convert between parametric form and other forms (such as rectangular/Cartesian form); graph curves in parametric form, including using technology to do so.
09.02 Polar Curves Analyze planar curves given in polar form; be able to compute derivatives of polar functions and their applications such as finding tangent lines; find the area between polar curves; convert between polar form and other forms (such as rectangular/Cartesian form); graph curves in polar form, including using technology to do so.
09.03 Vector Curves Analyze planar curves given in vector form; be able to compute derivatives of vector functions, including their application in rectilinear motion and calculating related properties such as distance, displacement, velocity, acceleration, jerk, etc.
09.04 Length of Planar Curves Analyze planar curves given in parametric form; be able to compute derivatives of parametric functions and their applications such as finding tangent lines; find the arc length of a curve described in Cartesian form as well as parametric form; convert between parametric form and other forms (such as rectangular/Cartesian form); graph curves in parametric form, including using technology to do so.
09.05 Area of Planar Curves (Polar Curves only) Analyze planar curves given in polar form; be able to compute derivatives of polar functions and their applications such as finding tangent lines; find the area between polar curves; convert between polar form and other forms (such as rectangular/Cartesian form); graph curves in polar form, including using technology to do so.
