Student Contact and Drop Policy

Your online school sets high expectations for the success of its students. We are committed to meeting the needs of all students and will work tirelessly to facilitate student success.

Credit Recovery course requirements:

  • required attendance and 10% progress each week
  • mandatory weekly live Elluminate sessions
  • 14 day grace period
  • student must have failed course
  • credit recovery courses will not replace a student's grade
  • credit recovery courses are not NCAA approved

Attendance Policy

First week without attendance

  • Instructor will communicate via phone and email that student has not worked in one week. Communication will reflect that student must contact instructor to revise pace chart and that failure to adhere to revised pace chart will result in withdrawal.
  • Student will need to complete an accelerated pace plan(in collaboration with teacher)
  • Plan will reflect number of assignments required to meet 10% requirement

Second week without attendance

  • Student Withdrawal
