Collaborative Lesson Instructions

Part 1:

Once you have completed the lesson material in this module, complete and submit the assigned problems to your instructor and then contact your instructor to complete a Collaborative Lesson. You will work together with your instructor and your peers to complete this lesson. You will be required to submit proof of participation as determined by your instructor.

Part 2:

Review the grading rubric for this assessment.

What to Submit?

Submit the assigned problems to 03.08 Discussion-Based Assessment or Collaborative Lesson.

Part 3:

Answer the following questions and submit in order to receive credit for completing the assessment.

  1. What date did you complete the Collaborative Lesson Assignment?
  2. What topics did you discuss during your session? Be as specific as possible.

What to Submit?

Submit the questions and answers to S1 Collaboration Component.

Note: The Collaboration Component is required once per segment.
