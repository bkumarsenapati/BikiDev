04.07 Absolute Value Equations Practice Answers

1. x = 8 and x = -8

2. x - 4 = 9 and x - 4 = -9
x - 4 + 4 = 9 + 4 and x - 4 + 4 = -9 + 4
x = 13 and x = -5

3. The answer is "no solutions" because there are no values of x that will give you a negative absolute value.

4. 2x + 3 = 9 and 2x + 3 = -9
2x + 3 - 3 = 9 - 3 and 2x + 3 - 3 = -9 - 3
2x = 6 and 2x = -12
x = 3 and x = -6

5. |x - 2| - 8 = 10
Isolate:
|x - 2| - 8 + 8 = 10 + 8
|x - 2| = 18

Write Two Equations:
x - 2 = 18 and x - 2 = -18
x = 20 and x = -16

6. 5 - |2x + 3| = -11
Isolate:
5 - 5 - |2x + 3| = -11 - 5
-|2x + 3| = -16
|2x + 3| = 16

Write Two Equations:
2x + 3 = 16 and 2x + 3 = -16
2x = 13 and 2x = -19
x = 13/2 and x = -19/2

7. 6 - 2|3x + 2| = -12
Isolate:
6 - 6 - 2|3x + 2| = -12 - 6
-2|3x + 2| = -18
|3x + 2| = 9

Write Two Equations:
3x + 2 = 9 and 3x + 2 = -9
3x = 7 and 3x = -11
x = 7/3 and x = -11/3

8. -2|x + 4| = 24
Isolate:
|x + 4| = -12

Since an absolute value would never equal a negative number, the answer is "no solution."
