08.10 Field Trip
Extreme Bedroom Makeover

Get ready to decorate your room!

You are going to give your bedroom a makeover on a $2000 budget. Be creative and have fun!

What you must do:

1. Make a scale drawing of your room so you'll know the area of each wall and the size of your room. Remember to find the area of a rectangle you multiply the base and height.

2. Create a budget for redoing your bedroom. You must account for all $2000. Please make sure you factor in labor costs unless you plan on doing the work yourself (make a note of that if so). Please list out each expense and give the final total of the cost.

3. Go to the discussion area and post your budget in the discussion folder titled "Extreme Bedroom Makeover." Please review at least one other budget and reply, pointing out the similarities/differences of their bedroom makeover budget and your bedroom makeover budget.

4. Write a paragraph describing your new room and include your measurements from your scale drawing. Be as detailed as possible and use proper grammar. Help me to "see" your room.

Assignment 08.10 Field Trip

What you need to submit:

  1. Your budget.

  2. The similarities/differences you found between your budget and your classmate's budget.

  3. The date on which you posted in the Discussion board and the name of the student to whom you replied.

  4. Your paragraph.

This assignment is worth 20 points.
