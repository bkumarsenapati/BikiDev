Academic Integrity (continued)

Academic Integrity = Academic Honesty

We expect a full commitment to academic integrity from every student and parent or guardian.

  • Academic integrity is a core value all students and parents or guardians must adhere to as part of the learning process.
  • The curriculum is designed so resources needed for student learning are provided within our coursework.
  • When referencing information in your writing, be sure to cite your sources properly.
  • Instructors are an important resource as well and should be contacted when student assistance is needed.
