Master the Art!

In this lesson you will learn how to manipulate the all-important exponential and logarithm functions. Being a master of these functions will help you in all fields that use even the slightest degree of math!

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • understand the properties of exponential and logarithmic functions
  • use a graphing calculator to explore the effects of changing any parameters of exponential functions on their corresponding graph
  • use a graphing calculator to explore the effects of changing any parameters of logarithmic functions on their corresponding graph

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Graphs of Exponential and Logarithmic Functions
  • Rules of Logarithmic Functions
  • Solving Exponential and Logarithmic Equations—A Few Examples
