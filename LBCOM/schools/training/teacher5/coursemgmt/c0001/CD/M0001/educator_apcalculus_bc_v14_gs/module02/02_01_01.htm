Know Your Limits!

This lesson will introduce you to limits, show you how to evaluate the behavior of a function based on its limits and how to graph it by hand and with a calculator.

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • use limits to describe the behavior of a function
  • use a graphing calculator to get numerical approximations for limits
  • evaluate the behavior of a function (in terms of limits) graphically (through sketching by hand and by using a graphing calculator) numerically and algebraically
  • understand the connection between vertical asymptotes and (infinite) limits and use graphing calculators to support conclusions

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Basic Introduction to Limits
  • The Concept of the Limit
  • The Definition of Limits and One-Sided Limits
  • Some Examples of Limits
