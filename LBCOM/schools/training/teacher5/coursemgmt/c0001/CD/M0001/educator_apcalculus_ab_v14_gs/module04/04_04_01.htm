According to Galileo, objects retain their velocity unless a force—often friction—acts upon them. In this lesson you will learn how to mathematically discuss this phenomenon.

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • use derivatives to discuss the motion and rate of change of objects in terms of distance and displacement, velocity, speed, and acceleration
  • use derivatives to discuss rectilinear motion
