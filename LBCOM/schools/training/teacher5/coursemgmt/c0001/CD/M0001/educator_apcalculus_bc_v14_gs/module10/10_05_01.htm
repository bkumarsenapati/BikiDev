How Good Is Good Enough?

Knowing how good your approximation is can be determined by using alternating series error bound or the Lagrange error bound. In this lesson you will determine the error bound of an approximation … really.

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • identify common series such as alternating series and the harmonic series
  • explore and compute the error bound of alternating series
