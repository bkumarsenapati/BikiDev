To Be or Not To Be … Convergent or Divergent

Once you are comfortable with the notation involved in series, defining convergence and divergence is easier. In this lesson you will determine what a sequence does and the application of geometric series.

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • use graphs and technology to explore convergence and divergence
  • determine whether a sequence converges (to a finite value) or diverges
  • explore examples that involve the application of geometric series including repeating decimal expansion
  • view terms of series as areas of rectangles and their relationship to integrals

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Convergence of a Sequence
  • Convergence of a Series
  • More Examples
