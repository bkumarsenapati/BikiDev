02.02 Graphs Practice Answers

1. Using the data from the US Public School Student Membership

a. What type of graph is shown? Line Graph
b. Make a statement about the school membership from 1990 to 2000. School membership has increased every year since 1990. Overall it has gone up by 6 million students.

2. Using the data from the Percentage of Students who Reported Being Bullied at School

a. What type of graph is shown? Bar Graph
b. What does the data tell us about the percent of students reporting being bullied by grade level? Students in higher grades report less incidents of bullying.
c. What does the data tell us about the percent of students being bullied from 1999 to 2003? At every grade level, the percentage has increased from 1999 to 2003. It has increased quicker in the lower grades.

3. Using the data from Airline On-Time Statistics and Delay Causes

a. What type of graph is shown? Pie Chart
b. What is the major reason for airline delays? How do you know? Weather, because it has the highest percentage (almost 75%) on the pie chart.
c. Make a statement about the relationship between airline delays and equipment. Equipment very rarely (only .2% of the time) causes airlines to be delayed.
