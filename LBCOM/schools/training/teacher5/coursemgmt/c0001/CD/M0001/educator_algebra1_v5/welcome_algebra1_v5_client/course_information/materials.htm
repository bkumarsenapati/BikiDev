Course materials

You will have access to Mathtype as provided by your school. Directions for downloading this product are located in the course information area of the course. Mathtype will allow you to type math symbols and equations to submit to your instructor.

Often students and parents ask for extra practice or examples to supplement the Algebra 1 online material. The online Algebra 1 course covers ALL the State Standards and National Standards and provides all the lessons that you will need to gain a comprehensive understanding of Algebra 1. There are times when extra practice can be both necessary and beneficial.

We recommend that you purchase the "Key to Algebra" Student Workbooks. You can purchase these booklets online at http://www.keymath.com. There are 10 work booklets and 3 solutions manuals that make up the Algebra 1 set. Below you will find a reference chart for your use of the booklets throughout the course.

These booklets are NOT a requirement of the course. Your instructor will not collect or grade any of the work that you choose to do in the booklets. The booklets will act as an EXTRA resource. By purchasing and using the booklets, you will gain a more solid foundation in Algebra.
