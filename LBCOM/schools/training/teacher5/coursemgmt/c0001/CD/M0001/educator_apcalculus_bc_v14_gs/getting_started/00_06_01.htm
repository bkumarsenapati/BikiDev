Pace: Moving Through The Course

Pace

In certain online courses, you are able to determine your own schedule or "pace." As an online student, you are expected to follow your course's pace and submit activities for grading in a timely manner.

Think about which pace would be right for you. Talk to your parent(s) or guardian(s), guidance counselor, and instructor to help you make the best decision.
