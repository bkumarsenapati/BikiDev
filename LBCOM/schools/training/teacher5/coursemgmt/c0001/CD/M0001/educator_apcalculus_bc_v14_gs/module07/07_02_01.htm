Let It Flow!

So, the flow of solutions comes from the direction field … which means your graph is going to look like an optical illusion now … just sayin'. In this lesson you will learn how to draw a direction field, interpret one, and interpret a solution curve. Pretty cool!

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • draw a slope field (or direction field) for a differential equation
  • interpret a slope field when given it
  • interpret the solution curve attached to an initial value
