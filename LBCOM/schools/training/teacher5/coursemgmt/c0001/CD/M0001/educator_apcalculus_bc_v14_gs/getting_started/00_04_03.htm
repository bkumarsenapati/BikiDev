Course Specifics: What You Need To Know (continued)

Discussion-Based Assessments (DBA)

Discussion-Based Assessments are a very important part of your course. You are required to complete at least one Discussion-Based Assessment during each segment of this course. A Discussion-Based Assessment is a conversation between you and your instructor. This is also a time to ask your instructor questions. There are opportunities provided to complete a Discussion-Based Assessment at the conclusion of every module in this course. For more information about completing your required Discussion-Based Assessment, please contact your instructor.

Collaborations

Collaborative Lessons are also a very important part of your course. You are required to complete at least one Collaborative Lesson during each segment of this course. For this lesson, you will work in a synchronous/asynchronous environment to master the module concepts. There are opportunities provided to complete a Collaborative Lesson at the conclusion of every module in this course. For more information about completing your required Collaborative Lesson, please contact your instructor.
