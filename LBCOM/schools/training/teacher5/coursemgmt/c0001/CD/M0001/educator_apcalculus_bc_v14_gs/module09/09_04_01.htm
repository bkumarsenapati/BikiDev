Danger: Curves Intensified!

A space curve is said to be a plane curve, if it is completely contained in a plane. In this lesson you will deduce the formula for the curvature and find the arc length.

Lesson Objective

After completing this lesson, you will be able to analyze planar curves given in parametric form; be able to compute derivatives of parametric functions and their applications such as finding tangent lines; find the arc length of a curve described in Cartesian form as well as parametric form; convert between parametric form and other forms (such as rectangular/Cartesian form); graph curves in parametric form, including using Technology to do so.


To meet this objective you will review the following instructional videos:

  • Arc Length of Parametric Curves
