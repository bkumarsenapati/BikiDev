01.03 Pace Assignment

01.03: Pace Travelogue

Working on a traditional pace schedule, it is recommended that you schedule a minimum of four days per week for 1.5 to 2 hours each day. Write out your schedule and share it with your parents or guardian. Put a copy of your work times in your notebook.

Assignment: Your job is to print out the Traditional or Accelerated pace chart for your class which can be found in the Course Information area. Put a copy of your pace chart in your notebook and post a copy in your work area. Give a copy to your parent, guardian, or lab facilitator.

Note: There is nothing to submit to your teacher for this assignment.
