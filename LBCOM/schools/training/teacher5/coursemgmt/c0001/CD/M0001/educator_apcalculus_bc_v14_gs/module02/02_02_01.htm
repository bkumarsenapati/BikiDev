Got Limits? Get Properties!

Knowing the properties of limits will make your life easier as you compute! In this lesson you will learn those properties and see examples of how they work.

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • evaluate the behavior of a function (in terms of limits) graphically (through sketching by hand and by using a graphing calculator)
  • evaluate the behavior of a function (in terms of limits) numerically
  • evaluate the behavior of a function (in terms of limits) algebraically

To meet these objective you will review the following instructional videos:

  • The Properties of Limits
  • Examples Using the Properties of Limits
