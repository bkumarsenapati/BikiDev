You will only be allowed to take this quiz one time. Once you enter the quiz, it will count as your submission. Here is a list of several situations in which you could be kicked out of the quiz thus using your only submission.

  • If you are not working constantly in the quiz then your ISP may kick you off-line. You cannot open the quiz and then walk away from the computer.
  • You cannot minimize your browser during the quiz.
  • You cannot move around in other parts of the course while the quiz is open.
  • You cannot work in more than one browser at a time while in the quiz.
  • You cannot enter the quiz, print it and then cancel out. Canceling out will still count as one submission and will send the quiz without any answers.
