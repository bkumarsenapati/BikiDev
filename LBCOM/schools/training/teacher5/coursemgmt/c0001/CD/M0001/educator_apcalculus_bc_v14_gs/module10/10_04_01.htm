Making the Impossible Possible

In this lesson you will continue to use various tests to determine the convergence or divergence of some series. Without using tests determining the convergence of some integrals would be almost impossible!

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to test a series for convergence or divergence using the:

  • ratio test
  • alternating series test

To meet these objectives you will review the following instructional videos:

  • The Ratio Test
  • The Alternating Series Test
  • Test for Convergence Summary
