06.04 FOIL Practice Answers

1. Here you must distribute the -5 to both terms inside the parentheses.
-5(2x) + -5(4)
-10x - 20 is the answer.

2. Here you must distribute the 3x2 to all three terms inside the parentheses.
3x2(3x2) - 3x2(x) - 3x2(2)
Remember that when you're multiplying you add your exponents.
9x4 - 3x3 - 6x2 is the answer.

3. Here you must distribute the -2y3 to both terms inside the parentheses.
-2y3(3x2y) - -2y3(6x2y2)
-6x2y4 - -12x2y5
-6x2y4 + 12x2y5 is the answer.

4. Here you must FOIL. Remember FOIL is an acronym to help us remember how to multiply two binomials: First, Outer, Inner, Last.
(x - 2)(x + 8)
x2 + 8x - 2x - 16
x2 + 6x - 16 is the answer.

5. Here you must FOIL.
(2x - 3)(x - 7)
2x2 - 14x - 3x + 21
2x2 - 17x + 21 is the answer.

6. First we must rewrite the expression.
(x + 5)2
(x + 5)(x + 5)
Now FOIL.
x2 + 5x + 5x + 25
x2 + 10x + 25

7. Here we must take each term from the first binomial and multiply it by each term in the second trinomial. We'll start by distributing the x and then we'll distribute the -3.
(x - 3)(x2 + 4x - 3)
x3 + 4x2 - 3x - 3x2 - 12x + 9
Now combine like terms.
x3 + x2 - 15x + 9 is the answer.
