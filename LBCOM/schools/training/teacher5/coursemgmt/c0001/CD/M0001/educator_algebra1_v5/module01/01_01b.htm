01.01: Motif and Course Information

Your assignments are called Travelogues. For this travelogue, you are required to get a Travel Diary (notebook) for this course. You will be asked to put a variety of hard copy items in your Travel Diary as you travel through the course. You should use this information to help you as you study for exams and work towards successfully completing Algebra 1.

You will find a specific list of items that need to be included in your Travel Diary in the Course Information area.

Note: There is nothing to submit to your teacher for this assignment.
