The Fight Is On!

Which explanation demonstrates best the concept of a derivative? You get to learn both ways and decide for yourself the answer to this historically argued question among the great mathematicians!

Lesson Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

  • understand the definition of the derivative as a local linear approximation and what it implies, as well as differentiability, and use graphs to explore tangent lines
  • understand the different notations for the derivative
  • explore the relationship between the graph of a function and its derivative
  • explore further the characteristics of the graphs of f and f'.
